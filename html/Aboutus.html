ABOUT US

FOUNDER

KAHUN SINGH

Welcome to our rootlife, we are one of the leading restaurants in Chennai, who welcome you to sit back, unwind and appreciate the lovely sights while our best gourmet expert sets you up for a scrumptious dining utilizing the best and freshest ingredients. The HOT 'N' SPICY restaurant's roots can be traced all the way back to 1972 founded by Mr.KAHUN SINGH.
He has always been a lover of food. He loved cooking and delivering them to his neighbours. After having such wonderful delicacies, the happiness on their faces hit on him so strong and that is how his career started to flourish.

His idea was to create an authentic North & South Veg and Non-Veg
restaurant specialized in multi-cuisine. He always makes sure of his authentic hand-made masala to be sprinkled on his recipes which highlights the taste. His dream grew bigger and bigger.. finally is now all over Chennai!!

With over 100 dishes on the menu, live cooking and multiple live counters, be it an office team outing, birthday or anniversary celebration, or a reunion with your friends after a long time, our chefs are waiting to help to make your day a magical one.

We have best chefs who pour their heart and soul into cooking. They are specialized in veg and non-veg recipes. From main course to dessert, every dish here is delightful and tasty. Our chefs are here to make you stress free with ultimate mouth-watering food. We are eagarly waiting for you to enjoy your meal and relax yourself!!

The main moto of our restaurant HOT 'N' SPICY is to make sure we have the best quality, exceptional service and warm hospitality, with a team of highly skilled & experienced chefs supported by a strong management team with different levels to ensure that our restaurant meets the standard.

With due emphasis laid on quality, service & hygiene, HOT 'N' SPICY is committed to Food Safety & Standards and strictly implements FSSAI : 2008 across the chain of restaurants and is periodically audited.
